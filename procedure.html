Step

Description

Image

1

Dishes were prepared by gluing silica sand
onto the surface with waterproof glue.

2

Planaria were placed into a half-rough half-smooth
petri dish for 15 minutes, and the side they ended up
on was their preferred side.

3

Planaria were put in a half-rough half-smooth
petri dish and placed under a light bulb for 15 minutes,
with their unpreferred side covered with black paper.

4

Planaria were placed into a petri dish
of their unpreferred side that was filled
with a 10% sucrose solution for 15 minutes.

5

Planaria were cut with a scalpel
and then allowed to regenerate.

6

Planaria were placed into a half-rough half-smooth
petri dish for 15 minutes, and the side
they ended up on was recorded.
